About Us

Vision AI is an AI-powered smart glasses solution aimed at empowering the blind and visually impaired to navigate the world around them. Through innovative features and community-building capabilities, Vision AI enhances accessibility and fosters inclusivity.

Problems faced by visually impaired

Reading Impairment

Limited navigation in enclosed areas

Difficulty recognizing distant people/objects

Decision-making hindered by environmental factors and context

Dependency on physical presence for assistance

Lack of contextual visual information

Get the Most with Our Glasses!

READ Feature

Vision AI's READ feature utilizes Context based Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology to convert text into context-based audio, enabling users to listen to text-based content such as mail, signboards, recipes, and more. This feature ensures that visually impaired individuals can access and comprehend textual information in their environment effortlessly.

Vision-based Authorization System

Vision AI has developed a unique and dynamic vision-based authorization system, ensuring secure access to the device and its features. This authentication mechanism utilizes advanced vision algorithms to authenticate users, providing a seamless and secure user experience.

Indoor Navigation System (INS)

Vision AI's Indoor Navigation System (INS) assists users in navigating indoor environments with ease and efficiency. By leveraging real-time location data and custom navigation algorithms, Vision AI guides users through complex indoor spaces, enhancing their mobility and independence.

POV Vision Streaming Application

Vision AI offers a Point-of-View (POV) vision streaming application that enables location-based volunteer selection with live location and video streaming. This feature allows visually impaired individuals to connect with volunteers within a specific radius, facilitating real-time assistance and support.
